2025 Dalhousie University Entrance Scholarship in Canada For Undergraduates | How To Apply

Dalhousie University Entrance Scholarship is a Women in Technology Scholarship which does not only offers financial support but provides a platform to support the next generation of female tech leaders and champions.

The Dalhousie University Undergraduate Scholarship provides a sum of $10,000 award for international students who are seeking to pursue an undergraduate degree at the Faculty of Technology.

Want more information about this opportunity? This post contains the details, eligibility criteria, benefits, and method of application for interested and qualified candidates.

Dalhousie University Entrance Scholarship Summary

  • Host Country: Canada
  • Study Abroad: Study in Canada
  • Category: Undergraduate Scholarships
  • Eligible Countries: All Countries
  • Reward: $10,000
  • Deadline: February 15, 2025

Dalhousie University Undergraduate Scholarship 2025 Details:

Dalhousie University is inviting applications from international and domestic applicants for the 2025 WeAreAllCS Women in Technology Entrance Scholarship.

Dalhousie University is a large public research university in Nova Scotia, Canada, with three campuses. It offers more than 4,000 courses, and over 200 degree programs in 13 undergraduate, graduate, and professional faculties.The university is a member of the U15, a group of research-intensive universities in Canada.

Computer science is about technology. More importantly, it’s about people. We need more female talent and perspective in the field and this scholarship is about supporting women wanting to have an impact within or beyond the tech community.

Through collaboration with our partners like The Leacross Foundation, The Women in Technology Scholarship initiative not only offers financial support but provides a platform to support the next generation of female tech leaders and champions.

WeAreAllCS Women in Technology Entrance Scholarship Requirements:

The majority of this Dalhousie University Undergraduate Scholarship will be awarded to individuals who:

  • Have an overall average of 70%, please see individual program pages for full admission requirements
  • Have applied for our Bachelor of Computer Science or Bachelor of Applied Computer Science program
  • Can demonstrate how a passion for technology has had, or will have, an impact on your community as defined in the broadest sense of the term.
  • Identify as female

The scholarship is open to applications from Canadian and international students.

Students who are Techsploration alumni or who have participated in the NCWiT Aspirations in Computing Awards are strongly encouraged to apply.

  • $10,000 scholarship ($5,000 entrance scholarship and $5,000 for year two of your studies (dependent on academic performance))
  • Access to events and professional development opportunities through the WeAreAllCS initiative and our partners

How To Apply for 2025 Dalhousie University Entrance Scholarship

Applications for Fall 2025 Dalhousie University Entrance Scholarship are now open!

  • To apply for the WeAreAllCS Women in Technology Entrance Scholarship, first submit an application to the Bachelor of Computer Science and Bachelor of Applied Computer Science in the Faculty of Computer Science.
  • Complete the WeAreAllCS Women in Technology Entrance Scholarship application form.
  • If you are eligible for more than one WeAreAllCS Diversity Entrance Scholarship, you will be considered for multiple awards through this form.

Applications close on February 15th, 2025.

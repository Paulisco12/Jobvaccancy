Bancor Outsourcing Limited is an outsourcing company that provides employee outsourcing and Management Services to companies that require such service(s) to enable them to focus on their cores and improve on their performance.

Job Title: Driver (Articulated Vehicles, Trucks & Buses)

Location: Ikeja, Lagos Employment Type: Full-time

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Maintain the service and appearance of vehicles daily
  • Maintain driver logs and recordall movements in the trip sheet
  • Safely transport consignment to customers' locations in a timely, safe, courteous and professional manner
  • Act as an observer during operations and report same to the control room and the operations officer.
  • Maintain vehicle Inventory which includes doing a general check of the vault to ensure all cash bags are removed after the day operation.
  • Adhere to all safety requirements particular to the vehicle, crew, consignment
  • Denying all unauthorized access into the vehicle.
  • Abide by all traffic rules and regulations including Federal Road Safety Regulations, LASTMA, VIO.
  • Follow delivery/pickup instructions from the supervisor

Requirements

  • At least 5 years of experience in driving articulated vehicles and trucks is a must
  • Experience in Cash management unit / Center as cash officer of a bank or Experience working with a Cash in Transit (C.I.T) organization.
  • Must know how to drive with clean driving record and valid driver's license.
  • Supernumerary (SPY) Police will be an added advantage.

Benefits

  • Salary: N80,000 - N100,000 Monthly. (Gross)
  • HMO
  • Pension
  • Group Life Insurance
  • Overtime allowance
  • Paid Time Off

Application Closing Date

31st January, 2025.

How to Apply

Interested and qualified candidates should send their CV or Resume to: recruitment@bancor.com.ng using the job title as the subject of the mail.

MorningStar Ceramics, a sales company involved in the selling of building materials, is recruiting to fill the position below:


Job Title: Sales Officer


Location: Amuwo Odofin, Lagos Employment Type: Full-time

Job Summary

  • We are seeking a passionate and capable sales officer to join our dynamic team and contribute to our growth.

Responsibilities

  • Drive sales of ceramic tiles, doors and sanitary wares
  • Identify and develop new business opportunities to meet sales targets
  • Build and maintain strong relationships with clients and stakeholders
  • Provide excellent customer service to ensure client satisfaction
  • Prepare and deliver product presentations and proposals
  • Monitor market trends and competitor activities to develop effective sales strategies
  • Handle client inquiries and follow up on sales leads

Qualification and Requirements

  • Bachelor's Degree / HND in Marketing, Business Administration, or a related field
  • Proven experience in sales
  • Strong communication, negotiation and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work independently and as apart of a team
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suites.

Application Closing Date

7th February, 2025.

How to Apply

Interested and qualified candidates should send their CV to: msconline@yahoo.com using the Job Title as the subject of the email.

Mojec Holding is a leading energy conglomerate with interest in smart metering solutions and renewable energy to support power distributions companies in Nigeria and independent energy generation plants.


Job Title: Front End Developer


Location: Mainland, Lagos Employment Type: Full Time

Job Summary

  • We are looking for a skilled Front-end Developer who is passionate about designing and developing user-centric web applications.
  • The ideal candidate will be technically proficient, detail-oriented, and able to translate user needs into seamless, interactive web experiences.

Responsibilities

  • Transform UI/UX designs into fully functional, interactive web applications with excellent user experiences.
  • Develop responsive and mobile designs to ensure seamless performance across devices.
  • Optimize applications for maximum speed, scalability, and performance.
  • Write clean, maintainable, and reusable code, accompanied by comprehensive documentation.
  • Collaborate closely with back-end developers and designers to ensure seamless integration and usability.
  • Monitor application performance, troubleshoot issues, and rectify usability problems based on feedback.
  • Create and maintain functional requirement documents, guides, and detailed mockups or prototypes.
  • Conduct browser testing and debugging to ensure cross-browser and cross-platform compatibility.
  • Stay current with emerging front-end technologies, frameworks, and trends to continuously improve development practices.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Web Development, or a related field.
  • Proven experience as a Front-end Developer, with a portfolio of live, interactive web applications.
  • Hands-on experience with modern frameworks and libraries.
  • Familiarity with the complete web development lifecycle, including design, development, and deployment.
  • Strong understanding of layout aesthetics and web accessibility standards (WCAG).
  • Exceptional analytical, multitasking, and problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.

Required Skills:

  • Front-end Framework: Proficient in React.js, Angular, Next.js, Tailwind CSS, Material-UI, and Chakra UI.
  • Core Technologies: Expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and experience with jQuery.
  • Performance Optimization: Skilled in enhancing web applications for speed and scalability.
  • Testing & Debugging: Familiarity with browser testing tools and debugging techniques.
  • Version Control: Experience with Git and other version control systems.
  • Design Tools: Knowledge of Adobe Suite, Photoshop, and familiarity with content management systems.
  • SEO Principles: Understanding of SEO-friendly development practices.

Application Closing Date

30th January, 2025.

How to Apply

Interested and qualified candidates should send their Resume with present monthly renumeration to: hr@mojec.com and: adewumi@mojec.com using the Job Title as the subject of the mail.

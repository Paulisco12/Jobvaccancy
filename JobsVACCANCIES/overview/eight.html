HBA MicroSupport Nigeria Ltd is a money lending company with focus on consumer lending. We are set to provide consumer loan to our target market in the most efficient way. We are a lending company with a focus on trade and consumer loans. We have been lending to our target markets for five years.

Job Title: Loan Officer

Location: Nigeria

Job Summary

  • Develop strategies to generate leads
  • Develop and maintain productive business relationships with prospects and customers
  • Analyse prospects and customers financials and make lending decisions.
  • Attend sales meetings as required.
  • Address customers' queries and concerns promptly and efficiently.
  • Follow up with customers on fund utilization.
  • Maintain required record keeping and reporting of all sales and after-sales activities.
  • Follow up on loan repayment in accordance with repayment schedule.

Requirements

  • OND / NCE / HND / BSc in Business / Finance / Marketing or a related field.
  • 1 - 5 years experience in a similar role in a lending company or Microfinance Bank in Lagos.
  • Ability to work independently.
  • Good written and verbal communication skills. Smart and articulate.
  • Good team spirit.

Salary / Benefits

  • Monthly Salary: Above industry average (N120,000 - N200,000) depending on qualification, experience and track records.
  • Generous Performance Bonus.
  • Health Insurance Package from a reputable HMO.
  • 13th Month Salary
  • End of Year Bonus
  • End of Year Gift.

Application Closing Date

11th February, 2025.

How to Apply

Interested and qualified candidates should send their CV to: tunde@hbamicrosupport.com using the Job Title as the subject of the mail.
